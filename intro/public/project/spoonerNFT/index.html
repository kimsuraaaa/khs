OWN 2 EARN

OWN 2 tEARN

In gaming NFTs, it is mostly a Play 2 Earn (P2E) structure in which NFTs are purchased and profits are earned through gameplay.
However, SPOONER NFT is a way to earn money just by holding the ownership of the game without playing it. We named it OWN 2 EARN in a way that provides profits just by owning the SPOONER NFT.
